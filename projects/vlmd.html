VIDEO LATENCY MEASUREMENT DEVICE

Another team project, developed in collaboration with a technology company.

The final product of this project is a device prototype that measures the real-time latency of a video stream from one location to another.

This was my final university project. It was also used as a basis for my thesis.

I worked with and tested different programming languages and microcontrollers to find the best combination for the purpose of the device.
